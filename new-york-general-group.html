Internal-Use Corporate Video for New York General Group.

Commissioned to produce a short form internal communications video for New York General Group, I developed a clean, business aligned animation to reinforce internal messaging.


The 30-second piece was designed to be visually cohesive, brand-aligned, and tonally appropriate for a corporate audience - balancing professional restraint with visual clarity. The final output supported internal briefings and demonstrated my ability to apply design sensibilities to strict business contexts.
